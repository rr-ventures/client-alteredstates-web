## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use neuroscience language to legitimize mystical experiences
- Balance "heart-opening" with "brain-science"
- Emphasize safety and expert facilitation
- Use "we" community language
- Include testimonials with specific outcomes
- Frame as "Rites of Passage" (transformation narrative)
- Highlight "safe, legal, drug-free"

**Don't:**
- Woo-woo language without backing
- Guru/cult energy positioning
- Overpromise outcomes ("guaranteed enlightenment")
- Make medical claims ("cures anxiety/depression")
- Compare to illegal psychedelics ("better than ayahuasca")
- Use singular "I" (Gene is part of a team)
- Ignore safety concerns

**Overall Tone:** Warm, credible, mystical-but-grounded
