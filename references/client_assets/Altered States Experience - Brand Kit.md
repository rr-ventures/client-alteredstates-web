# Altered States Experience - Brand Kit

This brand kit is designed to provide a comprehensive overview of the visual identity, typography, color palette, and brand voice for the **Altered States Experience**. It is structured to be easily ingested by AI coding assistants like Cursor to ensure consistent branding across web and application development.

## 1. Brand Overview

The Altered States Experience is a transformative event series that combines guided breathwork, live music, and the use of lion's mane mushroom to facilitate non-ordinary states of consciousness. The brand positions itself at the intersection of science and mysticism, offering a safe, legal, and drug-free alternative to psychedelic experiences.

**Core Themes:** Transformation, consciousness expansion, breathwork, mystical experiences, neuroscience, community, and healing.

**Brand Taglines:**
- "The psychedelic inspired breathwork experience guaranteed to open your heart and blow your mind!"
- "Altered States: Transformation and the Mystical Experience!"

## 2. Color Palette

The visual identity relies heavily on a dark, atmospheric base contrasted with vibrant, neon, and psychedelic accent colors. This mimics the immersive lighting used during their live events.

| Color Name | Hex Code (Approx) | Usage | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deep Space Black** | `#0A0A0A` | Primary Background | Used for main backgrounds, creating a dark, immersive canvas that allows neon colors to pop. |
| **Psychedelic Purple** | `#6B21A8` | Primary Accent | Used for gradients, glowing effects, and large atmospheric background elements. |
| **Neon Magenta** | `#D946EF` | Secondary Accent | Used alongside purple for vibrant, energetic highlights and light flares. |

| **Action Red** | `#DC2626` | Call to Action / Banners | Used specifically for high-impact text backgrounds (e.g., "BREAKING NEWS", "SAVE $500", or topic highlights in videos). |

*Note for Cursor:* When building UI components, default to a dark mode theme (`bg-black` or `bg-gray-900`) and use the vibrant colors for gradients, borders, hover states, and glowing effects (`box-shadow`).

## 3. Typography

The typography is bold, modern, and highly legible, designed to stand out against complex, colorful backgrounds.

### Primary Display Font (Headings & Titles)
- **Font Family:** Montserrat, Bebas Neue, or Impact (Geometric/Condensed Sans-Serif)
- **Style:** **Always BOLD and ALL-CAPS.**
- **Usage:** Used for the brand name ("ALTERED STATES EXPERIENCE"), event locations ("MONA VALE"), and major promotional text.
- **Treatment:** Often uses wide letter-spacing (tracking) for a cinematic, expansive feel. Frequently placed on solid color blocks (like Action Red) for maximum visibility.

### Secondary Font (Body Text & Subheadings)
- **Font Family:** Inter, Roboto, or standard system sans-serif (Clean, modern sans-serif)
- **Style:** Regular or Medium weight.
- **Usage:** Used for event descriptions, long-form captions, and UI elements.
- **Treatment:** High contrast (White on dark backgrounds). Keep line-height generous to ensure readability when discussing complex or philosophical topics.

## 4. Graphic Style & Imagery

The visual language is highly immersive, blending photography with digital art.

### Key Visual Elements
- **The Psychedelic Eye:** The primary brand icon is a close-up of an eye with an iridescent, multi-colored iris (green, purple, blue, gold). This should be used as a favicon or primary logo mark.
- **Sacred Geometry & Fractals:** Backgrounds frequently feature mandalas, sunbursts, and fractal patterns.
- **Prismatic Light Effects:** Lens flares, rainbow streaks, and glowing auras are used to create a "mystical" atmosphere.
- **Color Blocking:** Text is often overlaid on solid, brightly colored rectangular banners (especially red) to ensure readability over busy images.

### Photography Style
- **Event Atmosphere:** Dark, moody shots of large venues illuminated by massive, colorful wall projections.
- **Community:** Group photos of attendees, often outdoors or in natural settings, conveying connection and warmth.
- **The Setup:** Images of people lying on yoga mats with blankets, emphasizing comfort and the internal journey.
- **Sacred Spaces:** Photos of altars featuring Buddha statues, candles, and eclectic spiritual decor.

## 5. UI/UX Implementation Guidelines for Cursor

When generating code or designing interfaces for this brand, adhere to the following principles:

1.  **Dark Mode Default:** The application or website must be dark-themed. Avoid large areas of white or light gray.
2.  **Glow Effects:** Utilize CSS `box-shadow` and `drop-shadow` to create neon glowing effects around buttons or cards, mimicking the event lighting.
3.  **Vibrant Gradients:** Use CSS linear or radial gradients combining the Psychedelic Purple, Neon Magenta, and Electric Cyan for background accents or hero sections.
4.  **Bold Typography:** Ensure all `<h1>`, `<h2>`, and primary buttons use uppercase, bold sans-serif fonts.
5.  **High Contrast CTAs:** Use the Action Red or Golden Amber for primary buttons (e.g., "Get Tickets", "Join Now") to ensure they stand out against the dark, colorful backgrounds.
6.  **Immersive Hero Sections:** Hero sections should feature full-width, dark, atmospheric imagery with bold, centered text overlays.
